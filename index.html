About the Association

Dedicated to Supporting Your Success

Established as an independent, non-profit membership organization, the Professional Association serves both international and Cambodian enterprises united by a shared commitment to commerce and industry. Our mandate is to advance our Members' growth, foster cross-border collaboration, and contribute meaningfully to the Kingdom's continued economic development. We are proud to stand as a leading voice within Cambodia's international business community.
