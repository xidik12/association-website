About Us

We Are Here to Support You

Founded in 2005, the Professional Association of Cambodia is an independent non-profit membership organization dedicated to advancing professional standards and supporting the growth of qualified professionals across the Kingdom.

Our members include leading professionals, organizations, and institutions who share a commitment to excellence, ethical practice, and continuous development. Together, we shape the future of our profession in Cambodia.
