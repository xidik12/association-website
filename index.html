About the Association

We Are Here to Support You

Founded in [year], the Professional Association is an independent non-profit membership organization of both international and Cambodian companies who share a connection to professional commerce. Our role is supporting our Members' growth and success. Association businesses play an important role in the Kingdom's story of economic development and are a leading voice in Cambodia's international business community.
