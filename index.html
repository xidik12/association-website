About the Association

We Are Here to Support You

Founded as an independent non-profit membership organization, the Professional Association serves both international and Cambodian companies united by a shared commitment to commerce and industry.

Our role is supporting our Members' growth and success. Association businesses play an important role in the Kingdom's story of economic development and are a leading voice in Cambodia's international business community.
