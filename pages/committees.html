Ethics Committee

Upholding the highest standards of integrity, fairness, and professional responsibility across all member activities.

Meets monthly | Est. 2006

About This Committee

The Ethics Committee is responsible for developing, maintaining, and enforcing the Association's Code of Ethics. It provides guidance on ethical dilemmas, reviews complaints regarding member conduct, and ensures that all professional activities adhere to the highest moral standards. The committee also conducts outreach and training to promote a culture of integrity across the profession in Cambodia.

Key Objectives

  • Develop and regularly update the professional Code of Ethics and Conduct
  • Investigate and adjudicate ethical complaints brought against members
  • Organize ethics training workshops and awareness campaigns
  • Publish annual ethics reports and advisory opinions

Education Committee

Driving professional growth through structured training programs, certifications, and lifelong learning initiatives.

Meets bi-weekly | Est. 2006

About This Committee

The Education Committee designs and oversees all professional development programs offered by the Association. From entry-level training to advanced certification pathways, this committee ensures that members have access to world-class learning resources. It works closely with universities, training institutions, and international partners to deliver relevant, high-quality educational experiences.

Key Objectives

  • Design and deliver professional development workshops, seminars, and courses
  • Establish and maintain professional certification and accreditation programs
  • Build partnerships with academic institutions and international training bodies
  • Develop mentorship programs connecting experienced professionals with emerging talent

Standards Committee

Defining and maintaining the benchmarks of quality and excellence that govern professional practice in Cambodia.

Meets monthly | Est. 2007

About This Committee

The Standards Committee is tasked with developing, reviewing, and updating the professional standards and best practices that guide all members of the Association. It conducts research, benchmarks against international standards, and produces guidelines that ensure Cambodian professionals operate at a level of quality recognized both domestically and globally. The committee also manages the audit and compliance review process.

Key Objectives

  • Develop comprehensive industry standards and practice guidelines
  • Conduct periodic reviews and benchmarking against international standards
  • Oversee member compliance through audits and quality assurance programs
  • Publish technical papers, white papers, and standards documentation

Legal & Regulatory Committee

Navigating the legal landscape to protect the interests of professionals and ensure fair regulatory frameworks.

Meets monthly | Est. 2008

About This Committee

The Legal & Regulatory Committee monitors legislative developments, government policies, and regulatory changes that impact the profession. It serves as the Association's primary interface with lawmakers and regulatory bodies, advocating for fair and balanced policies. The committee also provides legal guidance to members, helps navigate compliance requirements, and produces position papers on proposed regulations.

Key Objectives

  • Monitor and analyze legislation and regulations affecting the profession
  • Represent the Association in consultations with government ministries and regulatory agencies
  • Draft and submit policy position papers and regulatory recommendations
  • Provide legal advisory services and compliance guidance to Association members

International Relations Committee

Expanding our global footprint through strategic partnerships with international professional bodies and associations.

Meets quarterly | Est. 2009

About This Committee

The International Relations Committee manages the Association's relationships with foreign professional organizations, international bodies, and regional networks. It identifies opportunities for collaboration, negotiates memoranda of understanding, and facilitates knowledge exchange programs. Through its work, the committee ensures that Cambodian professionals gain exposure to global best practices and that the Association is represented on the international stage.

Key Objectives

  • Establish and maintain partnerships with ASEAN and global professional associations
  • Coordinate international exchange programs, fellowships, and study tours
  • Represent the Association at international conferences, forums, and summits
  • Facilitate mutual recognition agreements for professional qualifications

Innovation & Technology Committee

Leading the profession into the future by embracing emerging technologies, digital transformation, and innovative practices.

Meets bi-weekly | Est. 2018

About This Committee

The Innovation & Technology Committee explores how emerging technologies can be leveraged to improve professional practice and service delivery. It monitors global tech trends, evaluates their applicability to the Cambodian context, and develops frameworks for responsible adoption. The committee also leads the Association's own digital transformation efforts, from online platforms to data-driven decision-making tools.

Key Objectives

  • Research and evaluate emerging technologies relevant to the profession
  • Develop digital literacy programs and technology adoption guidelines for members
  • Host innovation showcases, hackathons, and technology demonstration events
  • Lead the Association's internal digital transformation and platform modernization
