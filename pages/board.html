Board Responsibilities

The Board of Directors is entrusted with setting the strategic direction of the Association, ensuring sound governance, and upholding fiduciary responsibility to our membership. Board members serve voluntary terms and are elected by the General Assembly.

Strategic Direction

Setting the Association's long-term vision, goals, and policy positions on behalf of the membership.

Governance & Oversight

Ensuring compliance with the charter, approving budgets, and overseeing the Executive Director's performance.

Member Advocacy

Championing members' interests in dialogues with government, regulators, and international bodies.

Resource Stewardship

Safeguarding the Association's financial health and ensuring responsible allocation of resources.
