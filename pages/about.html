Our Story

About the Association

The Professional Association of Cambodia (PAC) was established in 2005 as an independent, non-profit membership organization dedicated to advancing professional excellence across the Kingdom of Cambodia. Registered under the Ministry of Interior, the Association serves as the leading voice for qualified professionals seeking to uphold the highest standards of practice and ethics.

With over 500 active members comprising individual practitioners, corporate organizations, and institutional partners, PAC plays a pivotal role in shaping policy, fostering education, and building a professional community that contributes to Cambodia's socioeconomic development.

Our work spans across six specialized committees, each focused on critical areas such as ethics, education, standards, legal and regulatory affairs, international relations, and innovation. Through these committees, we engage with government ministries, international bodies, and the private sector to create meaningful impact.

Purpose & Direction

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the foremost professional body in Cambodia, recognized regionally and internationally for its commitment to excellence, integrity, and the advancement of professional standards that contribute to national development.

We envision a Cambodia where every professional has access to the resources, networks, and support needed to achieve their full potential and drive positive change in society.

Our Mission

To empower professionals in Cambodia through advocacy, education, networking, and the promotion of ethical standards. We strive to:

  • Represent the collective interests of our members before government, regulatory bodies, and the public
  • Provide continuous professional development through training, workshops, and certification programs
  • Foster a strong network of professionals across industries and international borders
  • Uphold and advance ethical standards and best practices within the profession

What We Stand For

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do -- from the way we serve our members to how we engage with the broader community.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions, ensuring transparency, honesty, and accountability in how we serve our members and the public.

Excellence

We pursue the highest quality in professional practice, continuously raising the bar through rigorous standards, ongoing education, and a culture of continuous improvement.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together. By fostering partnerships among members, institutions, and international organizations, we achieve more than any one of us could alone.

Inclusivity

We welcome professionals from all backgrounds, experiences, and disciplines. Diversity of thought and perspective strengthens our community and enriches our collective impact.

Innovation

We embrace change and encourage creative thinking. From digital platforms to modern certification pathways, we continually evolve to meet the needs of a rapidly changing world.

Service

At our core, we exist to serve. Whether it is supporting an individual member's career growth or advocating for systemic change, our work is driven by a deep commitment to making a positive difference.
